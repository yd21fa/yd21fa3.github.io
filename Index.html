Tiger (Panthera tigris)

Natural Predators: None

Tigers are some of the most majestic creatures upon the face of the earth.
The tiger is also the biggest species of the cat family.
Sadly, many tiger species are endangered or already extinct.
The main reason for this is due to humans. We are constantly hunting tigers and trying to domesticate them.
In fact, there are more domestic tigers than there are wild ones!
While most cats are known for hating water, the tiger is the one cat that is an excellent swimmer and enjoys the water.

Diet:

  • Wild Boar Meat
  • Elk
  • Deer
  • Lynx
  • Bears
  • Rabbits
  • Fish
  • Rodents

Lion (Panthera leo)

Natural Predators: Hyenas (rarely)

Lions are known to be the "Kings of the Jungle." Their roar can be heard from very far away.
Most lions live in Africa. Unlike tigers, lions enjoy living together.
They live in what is called a "pride."
Also, while this may seem shocking at first, lionesses, female lions, do most of the hunting
Lions can also be very territorial.

Diet:

  • Antelopes
  • Buffaloes
  • Zebras
  • Young Elephants
  • Rhinos
  • Hippos
  • Wild Hogs
  • Crocodiles
  • Giraffes

Leopard (Panthera pardus)

Natural Predators: None

Leopards are also very interesting creatures.
Not only are they excellent hunters, but they are also great swimmers and climbers.
Leopards have really long tails and this helps them with their balance.
Like tigers, leopards are solitary animals and they are very territorial.
While some animals may bury their leftovers or hide it in a specific area,
leopards actually drag their food up high into the trees until they want to eat again.

Diet:

  • Gazelle
  • Impala
  • Deer
  • Wildebeast
  • Monkeys
  • Rodents
  • Birds

Jaguar (Panthera onca)

Natural Predators: Anaconda

Jaguars are pretty similar to tigers.
They are solitary animals and they ambush their prey.
Quite like tigers, jaguars also enjoy water and they are fantastic swimmers.
Jaguars are usually only found in America, but there are also some in South America.
Jaguars have a really strong bite so you better watch out!

Diet:

  • Deer
  • Peccary
  • Crocodiles
  • Snakes
  • Monkeys
  • Sloths
  • Tapirs
  • Fish
